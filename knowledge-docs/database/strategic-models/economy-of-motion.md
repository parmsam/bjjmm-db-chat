## **Favor techniques that require minimal movement and energy.**

When you have multiple options available, favor techniques that require less motion and effort. Moves that are simpler and require less motion:

*   rely less on athletic prowess
*   leave fewer openings
*   create fewer unpredictable "scramble" situations.
