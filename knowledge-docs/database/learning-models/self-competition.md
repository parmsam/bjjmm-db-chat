## **The only person you need to compare yourself against is the person you were yesterday.**

Comparing yourself against others, being jealous, and being overly focused on your competition are unproductive mindsets. The best way to ensure personal growth is to focus on ongoing self-improvement.

Even in competitive situations, individual wins and losses should not be dwelt on, but rather should be seen as feedback on your own journey.
