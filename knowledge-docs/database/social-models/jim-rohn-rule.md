## **You are the average of the five people you spend the most time with.**

The Jim Rohn Rule is derived from a quote by the legendary motivational speaker of the same name:

> **“You are the average of the five people you spend the most time with.”**
> 
> —Jim Rohn

There are infinite ways to become successful, but there's only one thing that all successful people have in common:

**Successful people surround themselves with other successful people.**

Nobody succeeds in a vacuum. The relationships you make, and the support structures you build around yourself, are the main variable driving your long-term success.

Personal growth will only get you so far…at the end of the day, **you need to surround yourself with people you admire.**

And this is hard to do. Changing yourself is hard enough…but changing the people around you means having lots of difficult conversations, and potentially abandoning toxic long-term relationships.

**You'll only be successful as the people you surround yourself with.** Make sure your relationships are empowering you, not holding you back.
